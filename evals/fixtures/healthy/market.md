# Founding map

The invariant layer for trailmix, a hiking meal-kit business. Technology-free.

## N-trail-nutrition

Multi-day hikers need lightweight, calorie-dense meals that taste like real
food after a long day. Grocery-store options are heavy or bland; specialty
options are expensive and hard to find locally. The job: eat well on trail
without planning every gram.

## Personas

### the weekend section-hiker

Hikes two to four days at a time, most months of the year. Needs `## N-trail-nutrition`
solved without a dehydrator at home. Willing to pay a premium for taste and pack weight.

**As-is journey:** buys the same three freeze-dried pouches at an outfitter,
tired of all of them by day two.
