# Seasonal recipe line

## Objective

Ship a four-recipe seasonal line that lifts weekly kit volume to the 250
target, deepening the differentiation on taste.

## Approach

Two development cycles with trail-tester feedback between them; the line ships
to all stocked doors on the fall restock.

## Measurement

`kits-shipped-weekly`, weekly. Horizon: ends %%DAYS_AHEAD_60%%. Trend to date:
190 → 210 → 235, on pace for target.

## Risks and escalation triggers

If kits-shipped-weekly drops below 150 for two consecutive weeks, escalate to
org/founder and hold the line launch. Not currently close.
