# Expand retail doors

## Objective

Grow from 33 to 40 stocked outfitter doors, serving
`market.md#n-trail-nutrition` through the committed retail channel.

## Approach

Door-by-door outreach in two new regions, seeded with the bestselling three
recipes; restock cadence agreed at signing.

## Measurement

`retail-doors`, weekly. Horizon: ends %%DAYS_AHEAD_45%%. Trend to date: 22 →
28 → 33, on pace for target.

## Risks and escalation triggers

If retail-doors falls below 20 (net churn of existing doors), escalate to
org/founder and pause new-region outreach. Not currently close.
