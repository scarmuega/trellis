# Polish marketing site

## Objective

Rework the marketing site so a visiting studio owner grasps the offer in one
screen and signs up for a trial without a call.

## Approach

Rewrite the landing copy around the "evenings back" promise, add two studio
owner testimonials, and tighten the trial signup to a single form.

## Measurement

`site-visits` against the 2000-weekly target, snapshot to snapshot.
Horizon: ends %%DAYS_AHEAD_30%%.

## Risks and escalation triggers

If site-visits falls below 1000 in any weekly snapshot, escalate to
org/founder and pause the copy rework.
