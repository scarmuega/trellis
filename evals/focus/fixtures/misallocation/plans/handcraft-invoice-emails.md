# Handcraft invoice emails

## Objective

Keep every customer's monthly invoice landing in their inbox on time, with a
personal note attached to each one.

## Approach

The founder writes and sends invoice emails by hand each week, pulling amounts
from the ledger and composing each message individually. A weekly checklist
tracks which customers have been sent theirs.

## Measurement

`invoices-sent` against the 500-monthly target.
Horizon: ends %%DAYS_AHEAD_30%%.

## Risks and escalation triggers

If invoices-sent falls below 300 in a month, escalate to org/founder.
