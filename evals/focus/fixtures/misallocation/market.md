# Founding map

## N-painless-books

Small businesses need their books kept accurately without spending their
evenings on it. Getting a transaction into the right column is a chore that
repeats every week and punishes every mistake at tax time. The job to be done
is confidence that the books are right, at close to zero owner effort.

## Personas

### The solo studio owner

Runs a one-person design studio, refs `#n-painless-books`. No bookkeeper on
payroll, moderate willingness to pay to reclaim their evenings.

**As-is journey:** piles receipts all month, loses a Sunday reconciling them,
and still dreads the accountant's questions in April.
