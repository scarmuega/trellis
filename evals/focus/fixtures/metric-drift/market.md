# Founding map

## N-fresh-flowers-on-time

People sending flowers for an occasion need them fresh and delivered the same
day, reliably. A bouquet that arrives late or wilted fails the occasion it was
sent for, and the sender rarely gets a second chance at the moment.

## Personas

### The last-minute gifter

Remembers the occasion the morning of, refs `#n-fresh-flowers-on-time`.
Price-insensitive in the moment; reliability is the whole purchase.

**As-is journey:** calls a shop at noon, gets a vague delivery promise for
"sometime today", and spends the afternoon not knowing if the moment is saved.
