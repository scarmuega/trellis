# Same-day delivery

The chosen means: orders placed before mid-afternoon are delivered the same
day inside a promised time slot, from local partner florists via our couriers.
The slot promise is the differentiation, so keeping it is the business.
It induces two core subdomains: running the courier operation that keeps the
slot promise (`problem/delivery-operations.md`) and building the order volume
that makes dense routes possible (`problem/order-volume.md`).
