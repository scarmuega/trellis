# Metric definitions

## store-revenue

Monthly gross revenue across all author storefronts, in USD.
Target: 8000 monthly direct revenue.
Owner: org/founder

## newsletter-subscribers

Total confirmed subscribers across all author reader lists.
Target: 5000 subscribers.
Owner: org/founder

## audiobook-conversion

Share of storefront visitors who buy the audiobook edition, in percent.
Target: 2% of storefront visitors buy the audiobook edition.
Owner: org/founder
