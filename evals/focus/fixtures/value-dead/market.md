# Founding map

## N-reach-readers

Independent authors need to reach readers directly, without a publisher's
machinery deciding who sees their work. The job is building a lasting,
addressable readership the author actually holds.

## N-earn-from-writing

Authors need income from their writing that does not depend on the whims of
retail platforms — rank changes or fee hikes can erase a livelihood overnight.
The job is a revenue stream the author controls end to end.

## Personas

### The midlist novelist

An established author with a modest back catalog and no marketing department,
refs `#n-reach-readers` and `#n-earn-from-writing`. Willing to pay for tools
that turn existing readers into direct income.

**As-is journey:** publishes through retail platforms, watches margins and
visibility fluctuate on decisions made elsewhere, has no direct line to the
readers who already bought the books.
