# Grow direct sales

## Objective

Lift monthly direct storefront revenue toward the 8000 target, serving
`market.md#n-earn-from-writing` through `strategy/direct-author-storefronts.md`.

## Approach

and roll winning storefront layouts to the rest of the fleet.

across the last three snapshots.
