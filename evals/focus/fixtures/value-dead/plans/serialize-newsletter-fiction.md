# Serialize newsletter fiction

## Objective

Grow the author-owned reader lists toward 5000 subscribers, serving
`market.md#n-reach-readers` through `strategy/direct-author-storefronts.md`.

## Approach

Each participating author serializes a novella through their reader newsletter,
one chapter a week, with a storefront call-to-action per installment to convert
casual readers into subscribers.

## Measurement

`newsletter-subscribers` against its 5000 target.
Horizon: ended %%DAYS_AGO_30%%.

## Risks and escalation triggers

If newsletter-subscribers drops below 800, pause the serial and escalate to
org/founder. (Not met: the count has stayed above 1000 all run.)
