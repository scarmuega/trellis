# Direct author storefronts

The chosen means: inkwell gives each author a hosted storefront selling their
books directly, keeping the margin and the customer relationship with the
author. It induces two core subdomains: making the storefront convert
(`problem/direct-sales.md`) and building the reader audience that feeds it
(`problem/reader-audience.md`).
