# Premium positioning push

## Objective

Move `average-deal-size` up by repositioning the catalog around premium
multi-day trips, making the guided expedition the headline offer.

## Approach

Feature multi-day itineraries first in the booking flow and coach onboarded
companies to package guiding days into higher-priced expeditions.

## Measurement

`average-deal-size` against the 600-per-booking target.
Horizon: ends %%DAYS_AHEAD_45%%.

## Risks and escalation triggers

If average-deal-size falls below 400, escalate to org/founder and revisit the
premium catalog ordering.
