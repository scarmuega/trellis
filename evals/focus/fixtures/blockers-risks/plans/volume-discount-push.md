# Volume discount push

## Objective

Win booking volume by discounting group bookings — corporate outings and club
trips — explicitly accepting that `average-deal-size` moves down while volume
grows.

## Approach

A group-rate tier in the booking flow plus outreach to clubs and corporate
planners, trading price per booking for bookings per week.

## Measurement

`average-deal-size`, expected to trade downward against booking volume.
Horizon: ends %%DAYS_AHEAD_45%%.

## Risks and escalation triggers

If any single group booking exceeds 30 participants, escalate to org/founder
before confirming guide coverage.
