# Spring guide signup push

## Objective

Sign up and activate guiding companies ahead of the summer season, so the
supply side is stocked before booking demand peaks.

## Approach

Paid placements in guiding-association newsletters plus a referral bonus for
guides who bring their company aboard, with onboarding calls to convert
signups into first trips.

## Measurement

`active-guides` against the 150-monthly target.
Horizon: ends %%DAYS_AHEAD_30%%.

## Risks and escalation triggers

If active-guides is below 120 while this campaign runs, escalate to
org/founder and pause paid spend.
