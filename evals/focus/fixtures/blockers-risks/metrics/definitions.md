# Metric definitions

## booking-throughput

Bookings closed through the pipeline per week — signed, paid, scheduled.
Target: 80 bookings per week through the pipeline.
Owner: org/founder

## active-guides

Guides who ran at least one trip on the platform in the last month.
Target: 150 guides active monthly.
Owner: org/founder

## average-deal-size

Average revenue per closed booking, in dollars.
Target: 600 per booking.
Owner: org/founder
