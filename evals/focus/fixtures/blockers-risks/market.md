# Founding map

## N-manage-bookings

Guiding companies need bookings, waivers, and payments handled without office
hours. The work that pays happens on the mountain, so every trip sold from a
desk is a trip not guided. The job to be done is a booking that closes itself —
signed, paid, and scheduled — while the operator is out with clients.

## Personas

### The mountain-guide owner-operator

Guides by day and runs the company by night, refs `#n-manage-bookings`.
No office staff; high willingness to pay for anything that closes bookings
unattended.

**As-is journey:** returns from a trip to a full inbox, chases waivers and
deposits by phone, and loses bookings to whoever answered first.
