# Guide-first CRM

The chosen means: campfire-crm runs the whole booking — inquiry, waiver,
payment, roster — so the owner-operator sells trips from the trailhead.
It induces two core subdomains — the booking pipeline itself
(`problem/booking-pipeline.md`) and getting guides onto the platform
(`problem/guide-onboarding.md`) — plus supporting pricing experiments
(`problem/pricing-experiments.md`).
