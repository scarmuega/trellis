# Metric definitions

## subscriber-count

Active paying subscribers at the end of the snapshot week.
Target: 500 active subscribers.
Owner: org/founder

## churn-rate

Share of subscribers cancelling within the snapshot month.
Target: below 5% monthly.
Owner: org/founder

## recipe-completions

Batches brewed to completion through a guided recipe in the snapshot month.
Target: 200 completed brews per month.
Owner: org/founder
