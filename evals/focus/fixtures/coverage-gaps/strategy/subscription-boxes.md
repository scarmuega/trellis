# Subscription boxes

The chosen means: a recurring box of batch-fresh ingredients, portioned per
recipe, shipped monthly. Freshness at the door beats selection at the shop.
It induces two core subdomains: sourcing ingredients fresh at box economics
(`problem/ingredient-sourcing.md`) and growing the subscriber base that funds
the cadence (`problem/subscriber-growth.md`).
