# Founding map

## N-fresh-ingredients

People who brew at home need fresh ingredients arriving on a reliable cadence.
Stale grain and old yeast ruin a batch, and sourcing trips eat the scarce
evenings the hobby lives in.

## N-brew-guidance

Novice brewers need step-by-step guidance through their first batches. Without
it they stall at the first confusing stage, blame themselves for bad beer, and
quit the hobby.

## Personas

### The weeknight brewer

A hobbyist who brews after work, refs `#n-fresh-ingredients` and
`#n-brew-guidance`. Time-poor, moderately willing to pay for convenience.

**As-is journey:** plans a batch on Sunday, discovers a missing or stale
ingredient on Wednesday, loses the week.
