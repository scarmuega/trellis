# 0008 — Name: Trellis

## Context
The working name DDB failed the framework's own naming rule: an opaque acronym with
a polluted retrieval key (Doyle Dane Bernbach, DynamoDB shorthand, the BSD kernel
debugger).

## Decision
The framework is named **Trellis**. Tagline: "The structure your business grows
along." Subtitle: "A domain-driven operating model for businesses run by humans and
AI agents." Strategy: proper noun as brand, descriptive phrase as subtitle (the
Team Topologies pattern); the acronym never returns.

## Consequences
The metaphor encodes premises 1 and 6: the business is the living, searching thing;
the framework is the fixed shape that guides growth without being it. Translates
cleanly to Spanish. Pending before public use: clearance (GitHub org, domain,
EUIPO — known adjacent uses of the word in fintech/legal-tech to check).

## Alternatives rejected
Domain-Driven Business spelled out (compresses back to the acronym in practice);
Domus (architecture-magazine collision); Estancia (reads themed internationally —
reserved for content, not the framework).
